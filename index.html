Our Story

A Krunchy Corner
of Accra

Nestled in Narhman, Accra, Casa Krunchy is a restaurant that celebrates the richness of Ghanaian culinary tradition — elevated with continental flair and served in a space designed for comfort and connection.

From our loaded Jollof Platter to the beloved Casa Breakie Royale, every dish is crafted with seasonal ingredients, chef-perfected recipes, and the warmth only a homegrown kitchen can deliver.

We are disability and kids friendly, making us the perfect spot for families, solo diners, and everyone in between.
